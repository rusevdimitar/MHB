MyHomeBills.info privacy policy

This privacy policy sets out how MyHomeBills.info uses and protects any information that you provide to MyHomeBills.info when you use this website.

MyHomeBills.info is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

MyHomeBills.info may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page regularly to ensure that you are agree with any changes. This policy is effective from 01 November 2009.

What we collect

We may collect the following information:

• e-mail address
• password
• IP address of last entry
• user input

What we do with the information we gather

We gather this information to build our database and provide you with a service for calculation and management of your monthly expenses, as well as planning of the latter and comparing them with database generated statistics.

Further, we use the information entered in the users' profile only for the following purposes:

• Internal record keeping.
• We may use the information to improve our products and services.
• We will distribute to third parties neither your e-mail nor any data you have entered.

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure we have implemented suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Controlling your personal information

Every user is fully responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of one's username and password and the manner of their usage. The user acknowledges that providing them to third parties is entirely one's obligation.

We will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so. We will not use your personal information to send you promotional information about third parties.

You may request details of personal information which we hold about you. If you would like a copy of the information held on you please write to support@myhomebills.info. If you believe that any information we are holding on you is incorrect or incomplete, please write to or email us as soon as possible, at the above address.
